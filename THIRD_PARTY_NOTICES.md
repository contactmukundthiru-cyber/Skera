## Signature Database

The library signature database (`data/signatures/libraries.toml`) contains
fingerprints of third-party libraries. These fingerprints consist of publicly
observable API names, error messages, and mathematical constants. The
fingerprints themselves are factual data and do not incorporate copyrighted
expression from the libraries they identify.
